Welcome

Valley View Primary is an independent school providing an enhanced educational experience for primary school students in the heart of the valley.

Valley View Primary School as a world-class Christian, African school established in 2015. The school accepts boys and girls from Grade 0 to Grade 7 into the Pre-Preparatory( Baby View), Preparatory and Primary School.

Based in Bezuidenhout Valley , teaching is based on sound values and morals .Valley View Primary is an independent school providing an enhanced educational experience for primary school students.
